How to Play

Controls:

Drag the Space Shit to the left or to the right using your mouse

You can also user your keyboard arrow key to move the spaceship.

Use the screen use the keys "A","S" and "D" to press the Button 1, 2 and 3 respectively.

Objective:

The objective is simple. You must shot the falling enemy pressing the right button. If the answer is right, you will get some points. If you are wrong you will not lose any points but you must dodge the enemy.

If you survive the wave, another one will appear, even harder.
